Tick, Tick... BOOM!

  • 2021
  • Running Time 115 minutes
  • Produced by Lin-Manuel Miranda

Tick, Tick... Boom! is a musical that tells the semi-autobiographical story of Jonathan Larson, a young composer living in New York City in 1990. The show explores Larson's struggles with the pressures of time, success, and artistic ambition as he approaches his 30th birthday. The narrative is driven by the ticking sound in Larson's head, symbolizing the urgency of his life and the need to make a significant impact before it's too late. The musical delves into themes of friendship, love, and the pursuit of artistic fulfillment, offering a poignant reflection on the complexities of life and the importance of making choices that matter.
